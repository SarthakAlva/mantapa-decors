About Mantapa Decors

Mantapa Decors is a passionate creative studio that transforms vision into vibrant reality. Based in India, we specialize in delivering visually stunning and culturally rooted decor for film sets, processions, weddings, and more.

Meet the Team

  • Prakash Shetty – Founder & Creative Lead

    Prakash Shetty is the producer of the critically acclaimed films Ammachi Yemba Nenapu and Koli Esru. He is also a seasoned theatre director, actor, and singer, actively performing with the Rangamantapa team. Known for his strong artistic vision, he is widely recognized for his contributions to art direction and production in both cinema and theatre.

  • Champa Shetty – Co-founder & Design Expert

    Champa Shetty is an Indian actor, filmmaker, screenwriter, and voice-over artist primarily working in the Kannada film industry and theatre. Shetty is known for her women-centric films.

    Career

    She started her career as a successful voice-over/dubbing artist and won the Karnataka State Film Award for Best Dubbing Artist (Female) in 2007–08 for Kurunadu. In the early 2000s, she worked as a prime time news reader for Udaya News.

    Champa has appeared in TV series like Kaveri (Nagathihalli Chandrashekhar), Nakutanthi (B. Suresha), Parama Pada (Yogaraj Bhat), and Mukta Mukta (T. N. Seetharam).

    She made her acting debut in Belakinedege and later appeared in Harikatha Prasanga / Chronicles of Hari by Ananya Kasaravalli.

    Her directorial debut Ammachi Yemba Nenapu (2018) was critically acclaimed. Her second film Koli Esru (Chicken Curry) won Best Film in the Indian Cinema Competition at BIFFes 2023.

    In addition to film and television, she directs Kannada theatre, including plays such as Gandhi Banda and Akku.
